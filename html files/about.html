About De' Cafe'

The Decaf Coffee Shop!

De’ Café, the decaf coffee shop was created not only to offer decaf coffee lovers a place but also to empower women. The idea for De' Café came from owner Caral Martin, an avid decaffeinated coffee drinker. She grew tired of the limited options for those who chose to drink; if there was an option at all. She decided to open a coffee shop that gave decaf lovers more than just one option. At De’ Café the decaf coffee is always on the top shelves, as opposed to the bottom shelf like it is in other shops. Caffeinated coffee is available in select varieties.

Our coffee is sourced sustainable and is certified organic and Fairtrade. We work to support female growers in many different regions including Myanmar, Papa New Guinea and Kenya. Our goal is to not only source the best coffee but also to help build strong families and economies.

Once our coffee has been purchased at a fair market rate from our growers, the beans our roasted locally in a state of the art facility. In order to give our customers the freshest decaf coffee, our beans are roasted in small batches.

Conveniently located north of Seattle, street parking as well as a small parking lot on the South side of the building are available for your convenience.
