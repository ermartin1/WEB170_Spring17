De' Cafe'

The Decaf Coffee Shop!

De’ Café is a decaf coffee shop offering a wide variety of decaffeinated coffee beverages as well as whole bean coffee. Caffeinated coffee is also available upon request. At De’ Café we strive to offer organic fair-trade coffee that not only provides income and opportunity for our growers but also supports our local economy. We carry many of the local coffee shops decaf coffees as well as our own branded coffee. Stop in and enjoy our ample seating or grab your Joe to go!

Conveniently located in north Seattle, street parking as well as a small parking lot on the South side of the building are available for your convenience.
